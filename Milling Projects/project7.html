Summary

The mind is the scene of the crime. In order to regain his former life, Don Cobb must succeed in convincing the son of an oil tycoon to break up his father's empire. However, incepting an idea in a person is thought to be impossible by all except for Don. Don ensembles an elite team to do the task, but the memory of his deceased wife may cause the whole dream to collapse.

Lead Actor/Actress Bio

Leonardo DiCaprio - Born in 11 November, 1974 in Los Angeles, California, Leonardo has had a diverse career. Starting on TV with such shows as Roseanne and Growing Pains. His breakout role was in "What's Eating Gilbert Grape", and became a star for the hit "Titanic". From there he has had multiple hits: "Revolutionary Road", "Aviator", "Catch Me IF You Can", "The Departe", "Shutter Island", and "The Great Gatsby".

Ellen Page - Born on February 21, 1987 in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Ellen had her breakout role in "Juno" (2007). In the movie, she played a cool quirky pregnant high schooler. She then went on to the lead role in "Whip It". From there she has starred in movies such as Touch Feely" and the upcoming "X-Men: Days of Future Past".
